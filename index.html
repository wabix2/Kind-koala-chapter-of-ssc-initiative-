First Semester in Review

Highlights from Hidar, Tahisas, and Tir — our chapter has been busy!

Hidar 22-26

Member registration drive · Health Facts Wednesday: rare medical cases during line-up · Assigned groups present fun health topics · Myth-busting sessions.

Tahisas (Week 2-3)

Tena Medhin research · Contacted Arada Mesob Health Office · General meeting · Health Facts by Groups 1 & 2 · Proposals and school letters submitted.

We Care Week (Tahisas Week 4)

Teacher appreciation (3 days): students taught periods, provided snacks & water for grades 9, 10, 11. All sections participated.

No Sugar Week (Tir 21-25)

Poster campaign promoting healthy choices · Daily reminders · Healthy snack alternatives · Short awareness sessions during line-up.

Drink Water Week (Tir 26-30)

Hydration challenge (8 cups/day) · “Carry Your Bottle” reminders · Class hydration counts · Daily water facts · Encouraged water over soft drinks.

Semester Break Initiatives

Hospital/medical institute visit · Outreach to organizations · Prepared class posters with emergency numbers & essential health info (morning awareness).

Monthly Recognition

Best Member of the Month announced (Hidar–Tir) · Monthly reports submitted to Kind Koala Ethiopia · Organization follow-ups.
