Semester in Review

A look back at our recent milestones and impact-driven initiatives from the past few months.

Foundation & Health

Successful member registration followed by weekly Health Facts sessions. We focused on rare medical cases and interactive myth-busting during school line-ups.

Teacher Appreciation

The "We Care Week" allowed students to give back by teaching classes and providing hydration and snacks to our dedicated educators.

No Sugar Campaign

A high-impact week promoting healthy dietary choices through daily reminders and nutritious snack alternatives throughout the campus.

Hydration Challenge

Encouraged the student body to swap soft drinks for water, tracking hydration counts and providing daily water facts to improve well-being.

Outreach & Safety

Semester break initiatives included medical institute visits and the creation of emergency contact posters for classroom morning awareness.

Member Excellence

Continuous recognition of our "Best Member of the Month" and consistent reporting to Kind Koala Ethiopia to ensure long-term growth.
