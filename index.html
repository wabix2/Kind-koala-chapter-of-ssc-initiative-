Impact Roadmap

Reviewing our past milestones and future goals.

Semester 1: Completed

Hidar - Registration

Successfully registered new members and launched Health Facts Wednesday sessions during school line-ups.

Tahisas - We Care

Teacher Appreciation Week for Grades 9-11 and the start of research for the Tena Medhin project.

Tir - Health Weeks

Execution of No Sugar Week and Hydration Week to promote healthy dietary choices campus-wide.

Semester 2: Planned

Yekatit - Tena Medhin

Launching medical operations and distributing All-in-One Health Cards to our verified members.

Megabit - Bottle Bank

Starting the Koala Bottle Bank to support local plastic collectors and environmental cleanliness.

Miyazia - Care Kits

Distribution of Koala Care Kits and Gratitude Week involving anonymous thank-you notes.
